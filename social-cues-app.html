Common questions

Why is my login name different from the posting name?

Your private login proves who may authorize an account. Social Cues publishes as the selected public asset, such as a Facebook Page, YouTube channel, shop, or Company Page.

Why do some platforms require professional or business accounts?

Their publishing and analytics APIs are attached to professional assets. Personal profiles often cannot grant the permissions needed for scheduling, moderation, or insights.

Can I add another platform later?

Yes. Open Accounts, add the provider, then enable any related workspace capability from Settings.

What happens when I turn a provider off?

Social Cues removes that provider's workspace cache and token evidence. Reconnecting restores fresh provider data, but deleted cached history may not return.
